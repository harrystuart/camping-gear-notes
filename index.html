LumenPack Trail 45 vs NorthRidge Alpine 42

This page compares two backpacks based on multi-day hiking use.

NorthRidge Alpine 42 – Downsides

After several trips, the Alpine 42 felt increasingly uncomfortable. The frame stayed stiff and never really broke in, and pressure points became noticeable on longer days. While the fabric feels tough at first, stitching loosened over time in low-stress areas.

LumenPack Trail 45 – Comparison

By comparison, the LumenPack Trail 45 distributed weight more evenly and felt noticeably more comfortable over long distances, especially with heavier loads.
